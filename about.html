About Samanantar Nirman Sewa

Samanantar Nirman Sewa Pvt. Ltd.is Client-focused and growing Construction Company operated in Nepal. Founded by Chairman Mr. Achyut Prasad Kharel in September 1993, the organization is providing multi-disciplinary construction services over a range of building, road, railways, canal and other infrastructure in different sectors. Operating in Nepal, we pride ourselves on the relationships which we build with business partners, clients and suppliers and on the quality of our product and services. The company’s quality of performance has enabled it to be recognized as class ‘A’ construction company by Ministry of Physical Transport and Infrastructure. Serving its objectives with great competence, experience and potential, the organization is an amalgam of more than 320 managerial, technical and administrative staff. The company is well supported with all the required resources, with its own under construction office building in K.M.C- 10, Kathmandu with high quality necessary plants and equipment.
