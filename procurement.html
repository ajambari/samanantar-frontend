Procurement Section

Procurement is the process of finding and agreeing to terms, and acquiring goods, services, or works from an external source, often via a tendering or competitive bidding process. The main objective of procurement in any organization is to achieve cost savings. It helps organizations gain a competitive advantage by enhancing customer satisfaction, time-to-market and organizational efficiency. Procurement helps integrate the suppliers as partners to assist in the value creation process..
